Progression Rules
  • →Add 5–10 lbs or 1–2 reps when top set feels clean at RPE ≤8.
  • →Strength target: +10–20 lbs on key lifts in 3–6 months.
  • →Strength drops >10% → add 200–300 kcal immediately. No exceptions.
  • →Refeed every 4–6 weeks: 300–500g extra carbs for 1–2 days.
  • →Fat loss ceiling: 0.5–1 lb/week. Going faster costs muscle.
